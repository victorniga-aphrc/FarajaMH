# Sample conversation for end-to-end testing

Use this script to test the app: log in as **doctor1** (clinician) or use **Simulated** mode and alternate between Clinician and Patient, copying each line into the chat. For **Live (Mic)** mode, read the Patient lines aloud to test speech-to-text and suggested questions.

---

## Part 1: Opening and mood

**Clinician:**  
Good morning. How are you feeling today?

**Patient:**  
I’ve been feeling really low for the past few weeks. I don’t have much energy.

**Clinician:**  
I’m sorry to hear that. Can you tell me more about feeling low? How would you describe your mood most days?

**Patient:**  
Mostly sad and hopeless. I don’t enjoy things I used to enjoy. I’ve lost interest in meeting friends.

**Clinician:**  
Have you noticed any changes in your sleep or appetite?

**Patient:**  
Yes. I have trouble falling asleep and I wake up very early. My appetite is poor and I’ve lost some weight.

---

## Part 2: Anxiety and worry

**Clinician:**  
Do you find yourself worrying a lot or feeling anxious?

**Patient:**  
Yes. I worry about everything—work, family, money. I can’t switch off and my mind races at night.

**Clinician:**  
Any panic attacks, or times when you felt very short of breath or your heart was racing?

**Patient:**  
A couple of times I felt my heart racing and had to sit down. I get tense and restless a lot.

**Clinician:**  
How is your concentration? For example when reading or at work?

**Patient:**  
It’s bad. I find it hard to focus and my mind goes blank sometimes.

---

## Part 3: Severity and risk

**Clinician:**  
Have you had any thoughts of hurting yourself or that life isn’t worth living?

**Patient:**  
I have had dark thoughts sometimes, but I don’t have a plan. I wouldn’t act on them.

**Clinician:**  
Thank you for sharing that. Have you ever heard voices or seen things that others don’t, or felt that people were against you?

**Patient:**  
No, nothing like that. It’s more the low mood and worry.

**Clinician:**  
How would you say these problems affect your daily life—work, relationships, taking care of yourself?

**Patient:**  
Quite a bit. I’ve been missing work and I’ve withdrawn from my family. I don’t feel like doing much.

---

## Part 4: Closing

**Clinician:**  
Thank you. Based on what you’ve told me, I’m hearing low mood, loss of interest, sleep and appetite changes, and significant worry and tension. I’d like to suggest we do a short follow-up and consider some options that might help. Would you be okay with that?

**Patient:**  
Yes, I’m willing to try. I just want to feel like myself again.

**Clinician:**  
We’ll work on that together. Is there anything else you want to mention before we wrap up?

**Patient:**  
No, that’s everything for now. Thank you for listening.

## Quick test checklist

- [ ] **Real Actors / Simulated:** Alternate roles and paste lines; confirm transcript and suggested questions update.
- [ ] **Live (Mic):** Read Patient (or Clinician) lines; confirm partials/finals and suggested questions.
- [ ] **History:** Open History, select this conversation, confirm messages and delete (if owned).
- [ ] **Admin/Clinician dashboard:** View conversation, check transcript and disease likelihoods; confirm owner shows as username.
- [ ] **Symptoms / word cloud:** Confirm symptoms (e.g. sadness, sleep, worry, concentration) appear; clinician sees only their own data.
- [ ] **Reset / new conversation:** Use Reset; start a new conversation and confirm old transcript is cleared.
